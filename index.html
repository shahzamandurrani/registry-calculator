Registry Process Guide

FBR Active Taxpayer List (ATL): Your filer status determines the withholding tax rate. Check status at fbr.gov.pk. Being a Filer can save you 6–9% in taxes.
Key Terms
TermFull FormExplanation
236KAdvance Tax (Buyer)Withholding tax paid by purchaser on property acquisition
236CGain Tax (Seller)Withholding tax deducted from seller on property transfer
TTipTown Tax / Transfer TaxMunicipal body tax on property transfer, fixed at 1%

Local Commission—Fixed ₨15,000 fee charged at time of registry
FilerATL TaxpayerFiled income tax return on time; qualifies for lowest rates
Late Filer—Filed after deadline; intermediate tax rates apply
Non-Filer—Has not filed a return; highest withholding rates apply
Step-by-Step Process
Step 1 — Verify FBR ATL Status
Both buyer and seller must check their ATL status on fbr.gov.pk to confirm applicable tax rates before proceeding.
Step 2 — Agree on Declared Value
Fees are calculated on the higher of DC rate or agreed market value. Undervaluation carries legal risk.
Step 3 — Pay Challan (PSID)
Fees are deposited via PSID at any designated bank branch. Obtain payment receipt before your registry appointment.
Step 4 — Attend PLRA Arzi Centre
Visit your local Tehsil office or PLRA Arzi Centre with CNIC, challans, title documents, and both parties present.
